Sustainable & Circular Production of Mineral Critical Raw Materials and Mining Waste Facility (SCIMIN_CRM & MWF) Ontology: Description back to ToC

It is an ontology for Sustainable and Circular Production of Critical Mineral Raw Materials and Mining Waste Facilities. It aims to represent the key concepts and relationships involved in the life cycle of Mining Waste Facilities (MWFs). By capturing this life cycle—from exploration and exploitation to closure—the ontology enables decision support and reasoning within the SCIMIN-CRM platform.
